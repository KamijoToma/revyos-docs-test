RevyOS

Introduction

RevyOS is a Debian-optimized custom distribution developed by the RevyOS team, supported by the RuyiSDK.

RevyOS provides comprehensive adaptation and optimization support for chips such as c910v, c920, c906fdv, and c908. It comes pre-integrated with the GCC toolchain supporting RVV0.7.1 and XThead, and utilizes glibc and thead-kernel optimized with the RVV0.7.1 instruction set.

Currently, RevyOS meets basic user needs in office work, web browsing, and video watching.

Based on these customizations and optimizations, RevyOS delivers excellent performance and a great experience on hardware platforms like Lichee Pi 4A, BeagleV-Ahead, and Milk-V Pioneer.
